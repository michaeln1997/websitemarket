Nunes' supermarket was created by Michael Nunes in 1990 and it has been 30 years in the business world. Michael Nunes created this supermaker about food around the world because when he was a young, he moved to the United States to get a better degree, so sometimes he wanted to feel like he was at home. However, he could not find any stores where he could find food from his country. This gave him an amazing idea to run this business which has been very successful since it is difficult to find special products from your own country in a different country which is very understandable. That is why Michael Nunes ran this business, so he could help people around the world to feel happy with excellent prices because he believe that it is better to sell cheap and more products than expensive and less products.
